                12. ## BCLB Regulation in Kenya
               
                13. The Betting Control and Licensing Board (BCLB) regulates all forms of betting in Kenya. Key regulatory points:
               
                14. - All operators must hold a valid BCLB license
                    - - The 2021 Finance Act introduced a 7.5% excise duty on betting stakes
                      - - Operators must verify players are 18+ before allowing registration
                        - - Responsible gambling measures are mandatory for licensed operators
